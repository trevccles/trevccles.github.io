Hyphen Trip 2022

Posted on November 13, 2022

The Hyphen group at my church (ages 18 to married), went on a trip this fall to the bay area. We stayed in beach houses for the weekend and went to the beach every day. We went on a boat ride in the bay, and walked around all the shops. The best part for me was taking sunset pictures on the beach with my friends.
